The Magic Formula

Your Knowledge + AI's Speed = Senior-Level Productivity

You give AI the context (codebase patterns, design constraints, edge cases).
AI gives you the execution (code generation, boilerplate, transformations).
Together, you're faster than either alone.

ü§ñ The AI-Paired Development Workflow

1

You Design HUMAN

Requirements, diagrams, edge cases. This is where your brain matters. AI can't know what the business needs.

2

You Prompt HUMAN

Break your design into clear, specific prompts. Include context from your codebase. Specify constraints and patterns to follow.

3

AI Executes AI

Generates code based on your prompt. Fast, but needs verification. Trust but verify.

4

You Verify HUMAN

Does it match your design? Handle your edge cases? Follow your codebase patterns? This is critical.

5

You Integrate HUMAN

Adapt AI output to your codebase style. Connect to existing code. Add what AI missed.

6

You Test HUMAN

AI often misses edge cases. Write tests yourself (or have AI write, then verify every assertion).
