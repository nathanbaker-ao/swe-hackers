üí° The Key: AI is amazing at generating options and documentation. You're amazing at judgment and context. Combine them.

üìã AI-Augmented Design Process

1. Requirements Synthesis

AI Heavy

Feed AI meeting notes, Slack threads, tickets, stakeholder emails. Ask:

  • "What are the requirements? What's ambiguous?"
  • "What questions should we ask stakeholders?"
  • "What are the implied constraints?"

2. Prior Art Research

AI Heavy

Ask AI about existing solutions:

  • "How have others solved similar problems?"
  • "What are common patterns for this type of system?"
  • "What are the known pitfalls?"

3. Design Generation

AI + You

Have AI propose architectures:

  • "Given these requirements, propose 3 architectures"
  • "What are the trade-offs of each?"
  • "What questions does each design raise?"

4. Trade-off Analysis

You Heavy

Apply your judgment to AI's options:

  • Which option best fits YOUR context?
  • What does AI not know about your org/team/constraints?
  • "What would we regret in 2 years?"

5. Documentation

AI + You

Let AI draft, you refine:

  • AI generates initial design doc from your decisions
  • You add context and correct inaccuracies
  • AI generates diagrams from your descriptions

6. Review Preparation

AI Heavy

Anticipate questions:

  • "What questions will reviewers ask?"
  • "What are the weakest parts of this design?"
  • "How would a skeptic critique this?"

üéØ The Art of Design Prompts

AI output quality depends entirely on prompt quality. Here's the difference:

‚ùå Vague Prompt
Design a payment system.
‚úÖ Effective Prompt
Design a payment processing system with these requirements: **Context:** - E-commerce platform, 10K orders/day currently, expect 10x in 2 years - Must support credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay - PCI compliance required, we don't want to handle raw card data - Currently on AWS, prefer managed services **Constraints:** - Team of 4 engineers, 3 months to MVP - Budget: $5K/month infrastructure - Must integrate with existing order service (REST API) **Questions to address:** 1. Should we use Stripe, Adyen, or build our own gateway connection? 2. How do we handle failed payments and retries? 3. What's our strategy for refunds and chargebacks? 4. How do we ensure idempotency for payment requests? Please provide 2-3 architecture options with trade-offs.

Prompt Patterns That Work

üéØ

Context First

Always explain your situation before asking for solutions.

üìè

Constraints Explicit

Team size, timeline, budget, tech stack ‚Äî all constrain solutions.

‚ùì

Specific Questions

Ask the exact questions you need answered, not vague asks.

üî¢

Request Options

Ask for 2-3 alternatives with trade-offs, not one "best" answer.
