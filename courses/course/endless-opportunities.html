About Endless Opportunities

Founded in 2011, Endless Opportunities has helped hundreds of first-generation college students in Greater Grand Rapids build competency, contribute to their community, and increase their economic capabilities.

This AI Freelancing Bootcamp extends that mission by democratizing access to AI tools, creating real income opportunities, and building confidence through practical skills that students can use immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access this course? +
This course is exclusively for students enrolled in the Endless Opportunities program. If you're part of the program, your coordinator will provide access credentials. If you're interested in joining, contact Endless Opportunities directly.
Do I need any prior experience? +
No prior coding or AI experience needed! We start from the basics and build up. All you need is curiosity and a willingness to learn. A laptop with internet access is required.
Can I really make money with these skills? +
Absolutely! Many of our students land their first paying clients during or shortly after the program. The skills you learn are in high demand, and AI gives you a major competitive advantage. We teach you exactly how to find and pitch clients.
What tools will I learn? +
You'll master 5+ professional AI tools including ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, DALL-E, Canva AI, and more. All core tools we teach have free tiers, so you won't need to pay for subscriptions to get started.
How is the course delivered? +
The bootcamp includes weekly 2-hour live sessions (Wednesdays 6-8 PM) plus self-paced online content, hands-on projects, and community support. You'll work on real projects that build your portfolio throughout the program.
