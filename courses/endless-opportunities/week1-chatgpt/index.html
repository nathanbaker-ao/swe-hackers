ü§ñ What is AI, Really?

Let's cut through the hype and jargon. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is simply software that can learn from examples and make predictions or generate content.

Think of it like this: AI is like a really smart autocomplete. When you text your friends, your phone suggests the next word. ChatGPT does the same thing, but it's been trained on so much text that it can write entire paragraphs, articles, and even code.

Your First Conversation

ChatGPT works like a conversation. You type something, it responds. But the quality of your output depends on the quality of your input. This is where prompt engineering comes in.

üéØ Prompt Engineering 101

Prompt engineering is the art of writing instructions that get AI to give you exactly what you want. It's the #1 skill that separates beginners from professionals.

Bad Prompts vs. Good Prompts

Write something about coffee

This is too vague. You'll get a generic response that's not useful for anything.

Write a 150-word Instagram caption for a local coffee shop called "Morning Buzz" in Grand Rapids. The tone should be friendly and energetic. Include 3-5 relevant hashtags. The post is about their new fall pumpkin spice latte.

This is specific, actionable, and gives ChatGPT everything it needs to create something you can actually use.

The CLEAR Framework

Use this framework every time you write a prompt:

C
Context
Who are you? What's the situation?
L
Length
How long should the output be?
E
Examples
Show what you want
A
Audience
Who is this for?
R
Result
What format do you need?

  3. Compare the two outputs. Which one could you actually use?
